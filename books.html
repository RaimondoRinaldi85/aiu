Egghead: The Story of One Small Trout is a humorous look at the life cycle of trout. Egghead overcomes his obstacle of being the smallest trout in a big tank and saves the other trout from being eaten by the bully, Bubba. I have found that children often cheer for the underdog. Way to go, Egghead!
